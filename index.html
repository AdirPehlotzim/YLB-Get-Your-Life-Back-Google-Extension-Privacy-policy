Privacy Policy for YLB: Get Your Life Back

Data Collection

We access website content (text and URLs) to scan for inappropriate keywords and blocked domains. This scanning happens locally on your device in real-time.

Data Usage

Website content is analyzed only to determine if blocking should occur. No browsing history, personal information, or website content is stored or transmitted.

Data Storage

Only your custom blocked words and extension settings are stored locally using Chrome's sync storage. No website content or browsing data is retained.

Data Sharing

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data or browsing information. All content analysis occurs locally on your device.
